About Me

Hello, my name is Ethan Dias Alberto. I first was introduced to technology at the ripe age of 8
My first experience was assisting my uncle
in jailbreaking his iPhone 5. From that point onwards, I jumped headfirst into the ocean that was IT.
At first it was seeking the limits of the vast expanses of iOS jailbreaking. Then it became discovering ways to breach the walls in "secure" devices, with me setting my sights on a future job as an ethical hacker.
It was only a few years later, that I changed my mind and decided to wholeheartedly chase the tail of the lion
named "Software Engineering".
